## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze **Sales** and **Gross Profit** performance by comparing **Year-to-Date (YTD)** results with the **Previous Year-to-Date (PYTD)**. The dashboard provides business stakeholders with a comprehensive view of company performance across products, countries, customers, and time.

The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Business leaders need quick access to key performance indicators to answer important questions such as:

* Are sales and gross profit improving compared to last year?
* Which countries are underperforming?
* Which products generate the highest profitability?
* Which customers provide the greatest business value?
* How does monthly performance compare throughout the year?

This dashboard was developed to answer these questions through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Executive KPI Cards

The dashboard displays key business metrics, including:

* Gross Profit (YTD)
* Gross Profit (PYTD)
* Gross Profit Growth
* Gross Profit Margin (%)

These KPIs provide an instant overview of business performance.

---

### Country Performance Analysis

A treemap visualization highlights the countries with the largest decline in gross profit compared to the previous year, allowing management to identify regions requiring further investigation.

---

### Monthly Gross Profit Analysis

A waterfall chart illustrates how each month contributes to overall yearly gross profit by displaying positive and negative changes throughout the year.

---

### Product Performance

A monthly trend chart compares Gross Profit across different product types while also displaying Previous Year performance for easy year-over-year comparison.

---

### Customer Profitability Analysis

A scatter plot segments customer accounts according to:

* Gross Profit
* Gross Profit Percentage

This helps identify:

* High-value customers
* Low-margin accounts
* Potential growth opportunities

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights

* Gross Profit increased compared to the previous year.
* Overall Gross Profit Margin remains close to **40%**, indicating healthy profitability.
* Several countries experienced significant decreases in profitability and require further business investigation.
* Monthly analysis reveals seasonal fluctuations affecting overall performance.
* Customer segmentation identifies opportunities to improve pricing strategies and customer profitability.
